# Le Petit Français 🥖

A fun, beginner-friendly French learning app with a warm red-and-yellow café poster design.

## Features

📚 **Guided Lessons** — Learn 10 words per lesson with audio, then take a mini-quiz and earn ⭐ stars.

❓ **Endless Quiz** — Test yourself on all words, both French→English and English→French. Build a streak!

👂 **Listening Game** — Hear a word spoken aloud, pick the meaning.

🧩 **Pair Matching** — Match French words with their English twins.

💬 **Survival Phrases** — Common phrases + pronunciation hints.

🏆 **Progress Tracker** — Earn XP, climb levels from Bébé Baguette to Légende Française. Your progress is saved.

🌍 **Bilingual** — Toggle between French and English at the top (choice is remembered).

## How to Use

1. Open `francais.html` in your browser (just double-click it, no server needed)
2. Pick a lesson and learn 10 words with native French audio
3. Take the mini-quiz to earn stars
4. Unlock endless quiz, listening, and matching games
5. Watch your XP grow and climb the levels!

## Tips

- Say words out loud — your mouth learns too!
- Make sure your Mac has a French voice installed (System Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Manage Voices)
- 5 minutes a day beats 1 hour once a week
